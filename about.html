Our Story

Built on real enterprise experience

Havlek was founded by Henry, a full-stack developer and digital marketer with over a decade of experience delivering enterprise-grade technology solutions across Canada, the US, and internationally.

What started as a web development and digital marketing practice has grown into a comprehensive digital agency — one that combines deep technical expertise with genuine business strategy thinking. We've built e-commerce platforms for national retailers, cloud infrastructure for telecom providers, mobile apps for international publishers, and dispatch systems for national service companies.

Today, we're extending that foundation into AI. Not because it's trendy — but because it's the most impactful lever available to businesses right now, and one that very few agencies are equipped to deliver practically and honestly.
