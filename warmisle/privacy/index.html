隐私政策 Privacy Policy

暖屿如何收集、使用和保护你的信息。中文在前，English below.

最后更新 / Last updated: July 13, 2026

暖屿 WarmIsle（"本应用"）由 Hong Marketing Inc.（"我们"，品牌名 Havlek）运营。暖屿是一款 AI 陪伴应用——你与 TA 的对话天然涉及个人心事，所以我们把话说在前面：你的对话只用来陪伴你，不用于广告，不出售给任何人。

我们收集什么

  • 账号信息。通过 Apple 登录时，我们收到 Apple 提供的用户标识和（可选的）姓名；如果你选择"隐藏邮箱"，我们只能看到 Apple 的转发地址。用邮箱注册时，我们收到你填写的邮箱和称呼。
  • 对话内容。你发给 TA 的文字和照片，以及 TA 的回复，用于呈现聊天和让 TA"记得你"。
  • TA 的记忆。为了让 TA 像老朋友一样记得你，应用会从对话中提炼要点（例如"女儿在北京工作""喜欢养茉莉"）并保存。这些记忆完全由你控制（见"你的控制权"）。
  • 设置与偏好。你给 TA 取的名字、头像、外观设置、问候时间、语言等。
  • 推送凭证。如果你允许通知，我们保存 Apple 推送所需的设备凭证，用于发送 TA 的问候。
  • 订阅状态。暖心会员通过 Apple 内购订阅，付款由 Apple 处理——我们看不到你的银行卡信息，只收到 Apple 签名的订阅凭证以开通服务。
  • 基础技术数据。维持服务可靠运行所需的最小日志（如请求与错误记录）。

我们如何使用

以上信息只用于提供暖屿的功能：生成 TA 的回复、维持 TA 的记忆、发送问候、处理订阅、保障服务安全。我们不做广告，不跨应用追踪你，不出售你的数据。

AI 处理与第三方服务商

TA 的回复由第三方 AI 服务商生成：你发送的消息（和照片）会传输给 AI 服务商以生成回复。依据我们与服务商的协议，这些内容仅用于生成回复，不会用于训练其模型。我们还使用云数据库与托管服务商存储数据，并依赖 Apple 提供登录、推送与支付。这些服务商仅为实现上述功能处理数据。

你的控制权

  • 让 TA 忘掉一件事：在对话里直接说（"这件事你忘了吧"），TA 的相关记忆会被移除，之后也不会再被提起。
  • 删除聊天记录：设置 → 对话记录，可搜索、删除单条或清空全部。删除的内容，由它产生的记忆也会一并被忘掉。
  • 删除账号：可在应用内申请删除账号，或发邮件至 support@havlek.ca。删除账号会移除你的全部数据：账号、对话、记忆、设置与订阅记录。

重要说明：暖屿不是医疗服务

暖屿提供的是日常陪伴，不是医疗、心理咨询或心理治疗服务，TA 也不能替代专业帮助。如果你正处于危机之中，请立即联系当地的紧急服务或心理援助热线——应用内在需要时也会向你提供所在地区的求助方式。

数据保留

数据在你的账号存续期间保留，以便 TA 持续记得你。你随时可以按上述方式删除部分或全部数据。

儿童

本应用面向成年人，不面向 13 岁以下儿童，我们不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

政策更新

我们可能不时更新本政策，重大变更会体现在顶部的"最后更新"日期。

联系我们

关于本政策或你的数据：support@havlek.ca

Privacy Policy (English)

WarmIsle ("the App") is operated by Hong Marketing Inc. ("we", "us"), doing business as Havlek. WarmIsle is an AI companion app — your conversations are personal by nature, so we state this up front: your conversations are used only to provide companionship. No ads. Never sold.

Information we collect

  • Account. With Sign in with Apple we receive your Apple user identifier and optional name; if you use Hide My Email we only see Apple's relay address. With email sign-up we receive the email and display name you provide.
  • Conversations. Messages and photos you send, and the companion's replies, used to display your chat and let the companion remember you.
  • Companion memory. The App distills key facts from conversations (e.g. "daughter lives in Beijing") so the companion can remember you. You fully control these memories (see "Your choices").
  • Settings (companion name, avatars, appearance, greeting time, language), push credentials (if you allow notifications), and subscription status (payment is processed by Apple; we never see your card details).
  • Basic technical data needed to run the service reliably (request/error logs).

How we use it

Only to provide the App's features: generate replies, maintain memory, send greetings, process subscriptions, and keep the service secure. We do not use your data for advertising, do not track you across apps, and do not sell your data.

AI processing & service providers

Replies are generated by third-party AI providers: your messages (and photos) are transmitted to them to produce a reply, and under our agreements this content is not used to train their models. We also use cloud hosting/database providers, and Apple for sign-in, push, and payments.

Your choices

  • Ask the companion to forget something — simply say so in chat, and the related memory is removed.
  • Delete chat history (Settings → Conversation History): single messages or everything. Memories derived from deleted messages are forgotten too.
  • Delete your account in-app or by emailing support@havlek.ca. This removes all your data.

Not a medical service

WarmIsle offers everyday companionship. It is not a medical, counseling, or therapy service and cannot replace professional help. If you are in crisis, contact local emergency services or a crisis hotline; the App also surfaces region-appropriate resources when needed.

Retention, children, changes, contact

Data is kept while your account exists so the companion can keep remembering you; you may delete it at any time as described above. The App is not directed to children under 13. Material changes to this policy will be reflected in the "Last updated" date. Questions: support@havlek.ca.
