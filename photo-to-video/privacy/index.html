Privacy Policy

How the Photo to Video app collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Photo to Video (“the App”) is operated by Hong Marketing Inc. (“we”, “us”), doing business as Havlek. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. The App is designed to work without asking for your name, email, or a traditional account.

Information we collect

  • Anonymous device identifier. When you first open the App, we generate a random identifier and store it securely on your device. It lets us maintain your credit balance and creation history. It is not your name, email, or Apple ID.
  • Purchases. Credit packs are sold through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes the payment; we never see your card details. We record which pack was purchased and your resulting credit balance.
  • Content you create. Photos and images you upload, and the text prompts you enter, are used to generate your videos. This content is sent to third-party AI processing providers to produce your result. Generated videos are stored so you can access and download them in the App.
  • Basic technical data. Standard information needed to operate the service reliably, such as app version and request/error logs.

How we use your information

We use the information above only to provide the App’s features: create and store your videos, maintain your credit balance, process purchases, and keep the service secure and working. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

Third-party service providers

To deliver the App we rely on service providers who process data on our behalf, including AI video and image generation providers, cloud hosting and database providers, and Apple (for in-app purchases). These providers process the content you submit solely to perform the generation and hosting functions described above.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete your account and associated data at any time from within the App (Profile → Delete Account). This removes your account, credit balance, and creation history.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: support@havlek.ca.
